Welcome to the Abbey!

Gather round, sisters and brothers, let our choir rise high and move the venerable old walls of our Abbey. It will be another long day of work for the Flüüfff monks. There is beer to brew, experiences to share, many games to play and way, way too many waffles to nom…

After the success of last year, the Flüüfff crew is happy to welcome you for the second edition of this event.

This year’s theme is “Welcome to the Abbey!”

We'll welcome you from the 31th of October to the 4th of November in the Corsendonk Duinse Polders hotel in Blankenberge.

~ The Staff

What is Flüüfff ?

It's Belgium's furry convention, located at the Belgian coast! Flüüfff is an event dedicated to anthropomorphic characters and a gathering place for all furries and their friends.
